About Globeks Corp

We're a veteran-friendly hot shot trucking company built on the values of reliability, transparency, and hard work.

Founded by drivers who know the road, Globeks Corp operates a modern fleet of 40-foot gooseneck trailers paired with heavy-duty trucks. We serve all 48 contiguous states with fast pickup times, real-time GPS tracking, and competitive pricing - no hidden fees, no surprises.

Whether you need a single car moved from Texas to Florida or heavy equipment delivered to a job site in Montana, our experienced team handles every load like it's our own.

Hot shot trucking is a type of expedited freight hauling using medium-duty trucks with flatbed trailers (typically 40ft). It's faster and more flexible than traditional LTL or full truckload shipping - ideal for time-sensitive or smaller loads.
